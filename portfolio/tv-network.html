TV Shows Network

Rate the TV shows you watch every day

This website was designed for TV lovers who don't want to waste their time watching bad shows. Here, users can search for the most liked series and the ones with the best reviews to choose one and watch it.

As mentioned, the site allows users to find, like, and comment on different TV shows. It fetches the catalog from the TVMaze API and uses the Involvement API to host comments & likes.
